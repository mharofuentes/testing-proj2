Introduction

The Yale School of Art is a graduate school that confers MFAs in Graphic Design, Painting/Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture; and offers undergraduate-level art courses to Yale College students. The School of Art is committed to the education of artists with a multiplicity of voices and experience and whose work is distinguished by its transcendent imagination, urgent dialogue, and dedication to the highest standards of excellence...

About Us

Welcome to the Yale School of Art, a vibrant and dynamic community dedicated to fostering creativity and artistic expression. Our graduate school offers Master of Fine Arts (MFA) degrees in Graphic Design, Painting/Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture. In addition, we provide undergraduate-level art courses to Yale College students, enriching their academic experience with a diverse range of artistic perspectives.

Our website serves as an ongoing collaborative experiment in digital publishing and information sharing. Functioning as a wiki, it empowers all members of the School of Art community—including graduate students, faculty, staff, and alums—to contribute, edit, and share content. This collaborative spirit reflects our commitment to inclusivity and the belief that everyone has a unique voice and perspective to bring to the creative dialogue.

The Yale School of Art embraces innovation and exploration in the arts. We encourage our community to push boundaries, challenge norms, and engage in meaningful artistic endeavors. Whether you are a current student, faculty member, alumni, or a visitor, we invite you to explore the rich tapestry of artistic expression within our community.

As we continue our journey in the world of art and education, we look forward to the diverse contributions and creative energy that define the Yale School of Art experience. Thank you for being a part of our collaborative and inspiring community.

Our Programs

Master of Fine Arts (MFA) Degrees

The Yale School of Art offers distinguished Master of Fine Arts (MFA) programs in the following disciplines:
  • Graphic Design: Explore the intersection of design and communication, pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling.
  • Painting/Printmaking: Immerse yourself in the world of traditional and contemporary painting and printmaking techniques.
  • Photography: Develop your artistic vision and technical skills in photography, from analog to cutting-edge digital processes.
  • Sculpture: Engage with three-dimensional art forms, from traditional sculpting to experimental installations.

Undergraduate Art Courses

Complementing our graduate programs, we offer a range of undergraduate-level art courses to Yale College students. These courses provide a foundation in artistic principles and allow students from various disciplines to explore their creative interests.

At the Yale School of Art, we believe in nurturing the next generation of artists, providing them with a solid foundation and encouraging them to push the boundaries of artistic expression.
